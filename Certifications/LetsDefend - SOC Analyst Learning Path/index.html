LetsDefend - SOC Analyst Learning Path

About

The SOC Analyst Learning Path on LetsDefend is a hands-on training program designed to teach the skills needed to work in a Security Operations Center (SOC). The course is structured into 25 modules that cover a variety of essential topics, including:

  • Basic SOC concepts and frameworks like the Cyber Kill Chain and MITRE ATT&CK.
  • Practical skills like analyzing phishing emails, detecting web attacks, and conducting malware analysis.
  • Core areas like network log analysis, SIEM basics, and incident management.
  • Advanced topics such as threat intelligence and building your own SOC lab at home.

The program is highly interactive, offering real-world simulations, quizzes, and SOC alerts to provide hands-on experience with tasks that analysts handle daily.

Learners can work through the program at their own pace, making it suitable for both beginners and those with some cybersecurity experience. At the end, participants earn a certificate to showcase their skills and improve their career prospects.

If you're looking for practical training to break into a SOC analyst role, this path offers a solid foundation combined with realistic exercises to build confidence in tackling real-world challenges.
